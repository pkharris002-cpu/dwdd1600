Digital Citizenship

What is Digital Citizenship?

Digital citizenship is the responsible, ethical, and safe use of technology and the internet. It refers to how people behave online and how they use digital tools to participate positively in society.

What is Digital Law?

Digital law refers to the rules and legal principles that govern how people use technology, the internet, and digital information. It sets boundaries for what is legal and illegal behavior online.

What is AUP?

An AUP is a set of rules that explains how technology, internet access, and digital devices are allowed to be used within an organization such as a school, workplace, or company.

What is Plagiarism?

Plagiarism is using someone else's words, ideas, or work without giving proper credit and presenting it as your own.

It doesn't matter whether it's done on purpose or by accident

What is Academic Dishonesty?

Academic dishonesty is any action that gives a student an unfair academic advantage or misrepresents their learning.

Academic dishonesty is any form of cheating or misrepresentation in schoolwork.

What are the rules for AI Generated Content?

Rules for AI-generated content usually depend on a school's or organization's policy, but most follow the same core principles.
